Welcome to My webpage!

I am Saman Bazargani, previously I was a Ph.D. student at university of Ottawa, under supervision of Vida Dujmović, Pat Morin and Prosenjit Bose. I was also a member of Computational Geometry Lab at Carleton University.

My research interests are as follows:

  • Computational Geometry
  • Graph Theory
  • Algorithm and Data structures
  • Combinatorics
For more information please see my publications or CV (pdf)
